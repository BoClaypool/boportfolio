About me

Originally intent upon a working in healthcare administration and having pursued graduate education in public health along these lines, I found myself very quickly seeking more productive freedom and earning capacity which brought me to working in physician placement. This was a natural match because it combined sales with my pre-existing healthcare experience. This has been a large part of my career focus ever since. In 2011, I was fortunate to be in a position to start my own business in this same space but much more narrowly focused on psychiatric prescribing. I viewed this as neglected area in the physician placement industry and I believed this area - psychiatry and psychiatric prescribing - would be an increased area of need forthcoming. In the time thereafter, this has proven true and in living this realization over a decade I have been granted a very intimate window into all aspects of virtually every behavioral setting in existence and in every US state.

As this profile indicates below, in addition to my primary focus, I have a number of various avenues of interest. Some are projects which are more than ideas but not quite proven or developed. This is not only professional inattention, but also indicative of someone who freely pursues their interests which I view to be one not only a huge privilege but a way to find joy in work.

If anything contained within resonates or piques any interest, do please feel encouraged to reach out. In so doing, I may find someone to partner with or - quite simply - a good person to know. I am most readily available to consult in the areas of executive search and private equity investment related to the behavioral health sector.
